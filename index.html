Purpose of this dashboard:

The primary purpose of this dashboard is to provide an interactive visual representation of data collected from multiple sources across the country with regards to bacteria that could be found in the human belly button. By selecting a given test subject ID from the dropdown, the user will be able to view the basic demographic information for that individual, the top 10 bacteria cultures found, the bacteria cultures per sample, and the frequency that the individual washed their belly button specifically on a weekly basis.
